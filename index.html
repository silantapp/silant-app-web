Silant

Everyone controls the music

Tired of having to manage music on your own?

Silant enables you to share control of the music thanks to a realtime collaborative queue.

Everyone can add their favorites songs to a shared queue!

You don’t like the currently playing song? No worries, vote to skip it!

You can as well save a song to find it later by giving it a like.

Enjoy all the songs from Spotify and Audius

A Spotify® premium account is required to use this streaming service. The music is played directly on the host's device.
